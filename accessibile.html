Early life

Picasso was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, on 14 March 1881. He attended the University of Buenos Aires and later studied at the Art Gallery of the University of Buenos Aires. In 1902, he moved to Paris to pursue his studies at the Saint-Paul-de-Mausole to become a sculptor.

Work

Death

Picasso died in Paris on 24 July 1973. He was 92 years old. He was buried in the Montparnasse Cemetery in Paris.
